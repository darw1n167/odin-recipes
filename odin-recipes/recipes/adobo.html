Chicken Adobo Recipe

Description

Chicken Adobo is a Filipino delicacy that is shared across the world. This savory dish will leave your mouth wanting for more.

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken thighs
  • Bay leaves
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup Filipino vinegar
  • 1 cup sugar
  • Handul of peppercorn
  • 3 cloves garlic and 1 onion
  1. Combine Chicken and Marinade ingredients in a bowl. Marinate for at least 20 minutes.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp of oil on high heat on a skillet. Begin to sear both sides of chicken until golden brown.
  3. Remove chicken and heat.
  4. With remaining oil, cook down minced garlic and chopped onions.
  5. Add reserved marinade along with the peppercorn and sugar. Simmer until syrup like consistency.
  6. Add chicken back and simmer for 20 minutes.
  7. Serve on top of a bed of rice and enjoy!
