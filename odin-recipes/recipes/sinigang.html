Sinigang

Description

Sinigang is also a Filipino delicacy known for it's unique sourness. Everyone needs to try it.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbs vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 tbs salt
  • 1 piece of fresh ginger, chopped
  • 1 lb bone-in pork chops
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 package tamarind soup base
  1. Heat vegetable oil in skillet on medium heat.
  2. Add onion; cook and stir until softened and translucent. Season with salt
  3. Stir in ginger, tomatoes, and pork chops. Cover and reduce hear to medium. Make sure to turn pork occoianally.
  4. Pour in water and tamarind soup base. Bring to boil then reduce heat and simmer until pork is tender.
  5. Serve on top of a bed of rice and enjoy!
